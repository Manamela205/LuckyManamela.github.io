Machine Learning Projects in Customer Analytics and Credit Risk Assessment

Project Overview: This portfolio showcases two machine learning projects focusing on customer analytics and credit risk assessment. The projects aim to address critical business challenges by leveraging machine learning techniques to predict customer churn and assess credit risk for individuals or businesses. Customer Churn Prediction: The first project involves predicting customer churn, which is crucial for businesses to retain customers and enhance customer loyalty. The project utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze customer behavior and demographics, identifying patterns that indicate potential churn. Credit Risk Assessment: The second project focuses on assessing the creditworthiness of individuals or businesses, aiding financial institutions in making informed decisions about loan approvals.
